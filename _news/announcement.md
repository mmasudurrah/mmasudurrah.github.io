📑 My paper titled "Automated Non-Invasive Burn Diagnostic System for Healthcare using Artificial Intelligence: AMBUSH-AI" has been accepted at [Annals of Surgery](https://journals.lww.com/annalsofsurgery/pages/default.aspx), **the world’s highest-cited surgery journal**. This paper proposes an innovative solution for burn diagnosis, including both human and porcine burn models. The AI model consists of a vision-language model that provides interpretable outputs for burn severity using multimodal ultrasound data.
